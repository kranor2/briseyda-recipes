Cookies Recipe

How to make Cookies

Have you ever wanted to make halloween cookies for a party or just for fun. Well today I will be giving you a simple and easy recipe. Down below you will see a nice simple recipe with nice and easy instrouctions to follow!

Ingriedients

  • flour
  • sugar
  • butter
  • eggs
  • baking powder/soda
  • vanilla extract
  • salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven
  2. Creaming the Butter and Sugars
  3. adding Eggs and Vanilla
  4. Mixing Dry ingredients
  5. Incorporating Mix-Ins
  6. Scooping and Baking
  7. Cool for 5 min
  8. Decorate
